Our mission

Autistic children may find filling in text-based forms difficult due to presumed context and vague questions. AvatarForms turns text based forms into interactive conversations with AI powered avatar. The AI can clarify the question, resolve specific user difficulties and skip questions that users do not want to answer. This helps autistic children to be more independent by eliminating teacher assistance in form filling.
